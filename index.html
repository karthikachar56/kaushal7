Founder Journey

The history, challenges, milestones, and future vision of EETIRP

Why I Started EETIRP

I noticed an alarming disconnect during my early academic career: while companies demanded practical exposure to software workflows, microservice architectures, and data design, classrooms focused almost exclusively on dry theory. I started EETIRP to build an interactive, hands-on student ecosystem providing automated code evaluations, AI mentors, and industry-oriented training.

Challenges Overcome

Launching a startup as a student in Bengaluru is demanding. We faced early difficulties deploying high-volume API registration loops, scaling candidate admission databases under heavy traffic, and designing lightweight algorithms that adapt to individual capability matrices. Standardizing backend routes using Express and MongoDB Atlas solved these database bottlenecks.

Key Milestones

Successfully bootstrapped the flagship student dashboard system, launched placement bootcamps in major institutions, and deployed multiple core apps. We have designed AI-powered placement evaluators, candidate screening systems, and smart scheduling trackers, successfully helping students verify credentials and master programming pipelines.

Future Goals

Our mission for the next 12 months is expanding the EETIRP ecosystem with nationwide bootcamps, automated candidate tracking networks, and native AI interview simulators (Butterfly AI). We are aligning university programs directly with tech recruiters to build a seamless, friction-free gateway for student hiring.
